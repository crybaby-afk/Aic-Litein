Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry at AIC Litein exists to disciple, mentor and mobilize our young people to know Christ and serve the community. We run weekly fellowships, community outreach, mentorship programs and skills workshops.

Activities

  • Saturday youth fellowship and Bible study
  • Monthly outreach and volunteering
  • Leadership & mentorship workshops
